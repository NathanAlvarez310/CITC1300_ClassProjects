Why are these are my favorite games?

I have found that these games have had the greatest impact on me. Whether it be emotionally, or simply how much fun I have had playing these games. I can tell you I have spent the most time playing Minecraft, all the way back in 2012 on Pocket Edition. I have found myself replaying Spider-Man on multiple occassions. The Jedi Order games have been my favorite Star Wars video games yet, their adaptation of Lightsaber combat is the best of any Star Wars games in my opinion. And as for Subnautica, even though I have yet to beat due to the scares it has given me, I stil enjoy playing it very much.

Of course I have many more games that I have played, I have a whole library of Steam games I have bought during Summer Sale that I haven't even begun to play. As is tradition. These games are simply put, as I stated above the ones that have left me with a great impact. But I will list some honorable mentions of ones that come close to my Top 4.

  • Roblox
  • Undertale
  • Star Wars: The Old Republic
  • Papers, Please
